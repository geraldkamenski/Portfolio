October 2025 - December 2025

Graphics Card
External Cooling System

This project focuses on the design and simulation of a compact GPU cooling system that uses forced airflow and a liquid cold plate to reduce thermal buildup in high power graphics hardware. Using CAD modeling and flow simulation, the system evaluates temperature distribution, airflow behavior, and cooling effectiveness to demonstrate improved thermal performance compared to conventional air cooling alone.

Submersible
ROV

Designed and integrated a small scale submersible ROV featuring a sealed pressure housing, multi thruster propulsion system, and Pixhawk based ArduSub control architecture with custom power distribution and wiring. Led electronics integration, system testing, and failure recovery, validating thrust performance, electrical reliability, and real time control through bench and in water testing.

November 2025 - December 2025

Jet Engine
Efficiency Testing

Designed and fabricated a small scale turbojet using aluminum can based components and off the shelf hardware, integrating a gravity fed butane fuel system and custom test stand for controlled operation. Instrumented the engine to measure thrust and fuel flow and used the data to evaluate efficiency metrics such as TSFC while iterating on combustion and flow path design.
